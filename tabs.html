Usage Guidelines

    Every page needs a breadcrumb that leads them back to the root of their actions.

    Tabs in the white bar should range from 2 - 7 options.

    The PureOHS logo should be clickable back to the Dashboard page.

    The cog symbol in the top navigation bar is meant to house all of PureOHS's set-up items.

    User profile related items should go under the person icon in the top navigation bar.

    The question mark icon should house all help and customer support related items.
